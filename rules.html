Contest Terms And Condition

The Participant must enter their details which include their Name(If Mandal, then Mandal Name),Contact Number,City, and State

For Video Contest,
The participants have to upload their video and thumbnail on our website, which then further will be uploaded to our Youtube Channel.
For Photo Contest,
The participants have to upload photos on our website, which then further will be uploaded to our Facebook Page.

For Video Contest,
The Winner will be choosen on the basis of Video Views And Video Likes on Youtube, for every video 1 view = 1 point and 1 like = 10 points. The top 3 Participant with the most point at the end of the tournament will be Selected in three different Categories (Home, Mandal, Eco-Friendly).
For Image Contest,
The Winner will be choosen on the basis of Photo Likes and Shares, for every 1 like = 3 points and 1 share = 5 points. The top 3 Participant with the most point at the end of the tournament will be Selected in three different Categories (Home, Mandal, Eco-Friendly).

Views, Likes and shares for all contest will be recoreded from 22nd August 2020 to 31st August 2020
After 31st August 2020 No likes, views and shares will be calculated

3 Winner from every categories will be selected for Both Photo Contest and Video Contest.
Name of the winners will be announced on 3 September 2020.
In the event the Participant is not able to utilize the Prize or is unable / refuses to accept the same, the Prize will be revoked.

Every Participant will get participation certificate.
In total of 3 Category first 3 winners will be awarded with certificate ,trophy and Cash Prize for Both Video Contest And Photo Contest

फोटो स्पर्धेसाठी,
सहभागींना आमच्या वेबसाइटवर फोटो अपलोड करावे लागतील, जे नंतर आमच्या फेसबुक पृष्ठावर अपलोड केले जातील.

फोटो स्पर्धेसाठी,
